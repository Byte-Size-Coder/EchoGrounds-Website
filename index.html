Why Communities Choose EchoGrounds

Purpose-built tools for Indigenous language revitalization and long-term community growth

Dictionary with Audio and Context

Document language knowledge in one place with indigenous spelling, translation, phonetics, categories, audio, and cultural context.

Guided Journeys and Assessments

Create structured pathways for learners in community programs and schools with progress tracking and assessments.

Expanded Resources

Preserve teachings and stories through long-form resources with translations and attachments for deeper language learning.

Support and Training

Provide ongoing support and training for Elders, teachers, admins, and learners to ensure successful implementation and usage of EchoGrounds.

From Word Bank to Learning Flow

Design journey lessons for learners to explore and engage with language through structured pathways.

  • Analyze and acknowledge a set path of words of a specific theme or category
