Transparent Pricing

One clear, all-inclusive model designed for Indigenous communities

EchoGrounds is built on fairness, clarity, and long-term sustainability. The all-inclusive price applies to core platform functionality, with clear caveats for high-compute AI and video services.

  • Unlimited users
  • Unlimited language content

  • Community data ownership

High-Compute Features Caveat

Some advanced features may be a potential additional cost depending on usage and compute requirements.

  • AI speech recognition
  • AI pronunciation analysis
  • AI translation or tutoring
  • Large-scale video hosting
  • GPU-based real-time features
The all-inclusive price covers core platform functionality. High-compute features such as AI tools or large-scale video hosting may be a potential additional cost depending on usage and compute requirements. Communities will always be consulted before any pricing changes, and no charges will ever be added without explicit agreement.
